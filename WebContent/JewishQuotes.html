The world endures for the breath of school-children. (Talmud Bavli Tractate Shabbat 119b)

This quote explains how important education is, and how our world revolves around it.

He who teaches a child is as if he had created it. (Talmud Sanhedrin 19a)

This quote teaches us how a person is not completely whole until they have been taught.

Both these quotes back up the importance of חינוּך, or the Jewish value of education.
